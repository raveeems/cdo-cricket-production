----- HOW TO USE -----

For Photo Mapping:
- Open players-export-full.csv in Excel or your photo mapping tool
- Match player_id (UUID format) with photo filenames
- Use player_name and team_short for visual reference
- Filter by role to organize batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders separately

Data Characteristics:
- All IDs are UUIDs (real synced data from cricket API)
- Credits: Standard player price in fantasy cricket
- Points: Current match points (default 0 for new syncs)
- Selected By: Number of users who picked this player
- Impact Player: Boolean - whether eligible for impact substitution
- Playing XI: Boolean - whether in official playing 11
