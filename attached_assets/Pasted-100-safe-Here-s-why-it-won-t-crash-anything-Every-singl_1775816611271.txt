100% safe. Here's why it won't crash anything:
Every single SQL statement uses CREATE TABLE IF NOT EXISTS — meaning if the table already exists, Postgres just skips it silently. If it doesn't exist, it creates it. Nothing gets dropped, nothing gets altered, nothing existing is touched.
Your existing tables (matches, players, user_teams etc.) are completely untouched. We're only adding two brand new tables.
Worst case scenario: the migration runs, fails silently (which it's already coded to do — look at the catch block in runMigrations), and your app starts normally as if nothing happened.
